Pavel Petkuns

Pavel Petkuns, known to his friends and fans as Pasha, is a freerunner from Daugavpils, Latvia. With his creative bar work and the easy fluidity of his movement, Pasha really stands out from the crowd

Pasha made a name for himself after the release of his first showreel in May 2009
which showcased many original moves. His creative and innovative take on freerunning has made him a master of urban spaces – the Latvian is always looking to create new tricks and has surprised his fans with his training methods

His dedication and originality make him one of the strongest freerunners in the world. Pasha has picked up two Red Bull Art of Motion titles and come very close to taking a third victory on Santorini Island. But for Pasha, it’s not all about the wins – he says that freerunning gives him everything that he wants in life – fun, food and friends
